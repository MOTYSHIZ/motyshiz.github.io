InVRasion

Asymmetrical 4v1 VR Multiplayer

Boss Death

Building Destruction

Air Mines | Consecutive Detonation

Face off against your friends as a giant robotic monster in a 1v4, VR vs NonVR battle! One player will control a giant robot via a VR headset, and up to 4 friends will team up against him/her for high-mobility split-screen action on the same PC or VR-enabled console.

Some features include:

  • An array of experimental weaponry.
InVRasion is my proudest work, and started off as my senior project for my Computer Science degree at California Polytechnic University of Pomona. After receiving academic credit for InVRasion, development has continued as a hobby since March of 2017. Created using Unity3D Game Engine, VRTK, and C#.

  • Date: January 2017 - Present
  • Category: Unity3D VR Game Development

Trouble In Terrorist Town VR (Working Title)

VR Networked Multiplayer via VRTK and UNet

This title is my foray into VR Networked Multiplayer with UNet and VRTK. Good tutorials on how to implement networked multiplayer in VR can be hard to find, so a lot of experimentation and research went into the initial setup of this. I do plan on moving from UNet to Photon in the near future, and one of my ambitions for this game is to include robust steam workshop support, so that modders can use it as a framework to make fun gamemodes, custom maps, and custom models.

[Current Version = As of 10/8/2018]
Features

  • Tracked VR Headset and hand controllers over UNet Multiplayer.
  • Tracked VR locomotion over UNet
  • Pickup of items via child/parent reassignment. This means that network tracking item transforms is not absolutely necessary.
  • Simple raycast weaponry that can be dual-wielded.
  • Health and damage system with a death state.

  • Date: September 2018 - Present
  • Category: Unity3D VR Game Development
